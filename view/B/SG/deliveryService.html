Delivery Options at Island Furniture

Parcel Delivery - $15

Order up to a weight of 40kg, and a maximum length per item of 1.8 meters.

Parcel size: (L) 59 cm x (W) 80 cm x (H) 60 cm

Example product: ROCKING (from children)

Delivery will take place within 3-5 working days, between 9 am and 6 pm.

Truck Delivery - $35

Delivery typically takes place within 2-5 days from the date of order.

Note: An additional flat rate of $20 will apply if there is no lift access, or if items cannot fit in the lift at the delivery address located in a building from the 3rd floor onwards. This service is applicable up to the 5th floor only. Please pay the fee directly to the service provider.

Drilling services: Only available with assembly purchases. Delivery-only purchases are not applicable for drilling services.

Please note that during major holiday seasons (Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, Deepavali, Christmas, etc.), please allow an extra 1-3 business days for processing time on truck and parcel delivery orders due to the high peak of orders.
