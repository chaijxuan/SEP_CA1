Assembly Services at Island Furniture

Easy and Professional Assembly

Our products are designed to be simple to assemble on your own, helping you save money. For those who need a helping hand, our professional assembly service is available at your home or office, ensuring a hassle-free setup.

You can schedule our assembly service during online checkout or directly in our stores, suitable for any product range, from a single piece of furniture to a complex cabinet system.

Once completed, we handle all the cleanup, taking away packaging and ensuring everything is recycled properly, leaving your space neat and tidy.

Assembly Service Fees

  • Complimentary assembly service for purchases exceeding $1000.
  • No additional fees for standard assembly.
  • A $5 charge per drilled hole applies only if wall-mounting is required and the assembly service was not scheduled.
  • Drilling services are available only when assembly service is requested.
